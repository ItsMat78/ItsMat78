## Things I've built

**[Lagrange-Lock](https://lagrange-lock.shreyashrai.com):** I got curious whether a learning agent could hold a satellite steady near a Lagrange point, where the orbit is naturally unstable. Turns out yes. The physics engine is Numba-JIT'd to ~10,000 steps/sec, which is the only reason training over 5M timesteps was tractable on my hardware. The live page lets you watch the orbit hold. *(supervised by Dr. Avantika Singh)*
`Python · PyTorch · Numba · Three.js` — **[live demo](https://lagrange-lock.shreyashrai.com)**

**[journal-advisory](https://github.com/ItsMat78/journal-advisory)** — A retrieval-augmented TUI over my own obsidian journal. Hybrid retrieval. Semantic similarity. Plus a recency weighting, feeding a multi-agent synthesis step. I use it to ask questions about my life, without losing context.
`sentence-transformers · ChromaDB · Python`

**[Taxshila Companion](https://taxshilacompanion.vercel.app/home):** Institutional management app, built solo and end to end. Live on the Play Store with real daily users and real database traffic. This one started as a problem my institution had. Solved.
`Next.js · TypeScript · Firebase`

**[Timetable generator](https://timetable.shreyashrai.com):** A small tool that builds conflict-free timetables. Live and usable right now.
`web`
